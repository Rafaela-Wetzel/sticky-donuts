Delicious Vegan Donuts in Leipzig

We are welcoming you in our newly opened Sticky Donuts Café in Leipzig-Plagwitz, the first vegan donut place in town! All our products are homemade with lots of love for your sweet tooth ❤

You can check out our delicious donuts and drinks in the sections below.

Come by Karl-Heine-Street, grab one of our sticky donuts and a drink and enjoy the sun outside on our terrace.

Hope to see you soon! :)

Our Donuts

We offer a variety of vegan donuts such as:

  • German style "Berliner"
  • Chocolate-Pistachio
  • Rainbow Sprinkles
  • Cinnamon Sugar
  • Almond-Walnut
  • Salted Caramel
  • Lotus Cookie
  • Tiramisu

...and monthly changing special donuts.

Our autumn favourite is a delicious chocolate donut with roasted hazelnuts on top. It surprises with a delicious not-too-sweet cream filling that balances the flavours and will leave you satisfied. Promised!



Don't hesitate to approach us if you have any kind of allergies and we will gladly provide you with information. We also always have two gluten-free donut options on the menu.

Drinks

We also have some tasty drinks to quench your thirst after indulging yourselves in our sweet delights. We offer:

Tea: Earl Grey, Herbal Blend, Mint-Ginger, Lemon, Chamomille, Oolong, Nettle

Coffee: Espresso, Americano, Latte, Cappuchino, Macchiato
